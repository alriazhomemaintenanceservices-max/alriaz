# Riyadh Area Marketing Strategy for Saudi Home Experts

## Area Segmentation for Smart Targeting

### Tier A - Premium Areas (Higher prices, quality expectations)
- **النرجس (Al Narjis)** - New money, villas, families
- **الياسمين (Al Yasmin)** - Established wealthy families
- **حطين (Hittin)** - Old money, established
- **الملقا (Al Malqa)** - Growing premium area
- **العقيق (Al Aqiq)** - High-end residential

**Strategy**: "فني متخصص للفلل" - Premium service angle

### Tier B - Middle Class Residential (Volume + Price balance)
- **غرناطة (Granada)** - Spanish-named districts, middle families
- **قرطبة (Qurtubah)** - Good volume, price conscious
- **اشبيلية (Ishbiliyah)** - Mixed income families
- **القيروان (Al Qirawan)** - Established middle class
- **الفلاح (Al Falah)** - Steady demand area

**Strategy**: "خدمة سريعة بأسعار معقولة" - Fast & affordable

### Tier C - Working Class/High Volume (Price sensitive, emergency focus)
- **الشهداء (Al Shohada)** - Budget conscious
- **الازدهار (Al Izdihar)** - High density apartments
- **الربيع (Al Rabee)** - Mixed residential
- **الندى (Al Nada)** - Apartments, quick fixes
- **المطار (Airport Housing)** - Transient population

**Strategy**: "إصلاحات فورية - أسعار مناسبة" - Emergency repairs, low prices

### Tier D - Peripheral/Special (Different approach needed)
- **الجنادرية (Al Janadriyah)** - Far out, farms/villas
- **بنبان (Banban)** - Industrial/residential mix
- **العارض (Al Aarid)** - Scattered population
- **الرمال (Al Rimal)** - Desert edge
- **المونسية (Al Munsiyah)** - Government housing

**Strategy**: "نصل لكل مكان" - We reach everywhere

## The Reality Check with 700 PKR/day Budget

You CANNOT effectively cover all these areas with $2.50/day. Here's what you should do:

### Week 1-2: Test Campaign
Run 3 separate campaigns targeting:
1. **Premium Campaign** (Narjis + Yasmin only) - 30% budget
2. **Middle Campaign** (Granada + Qurtubah + Falah) - 40% budget  
3. **Emergency Campaign** (All areas, emergency keywords only) - 30% budget
